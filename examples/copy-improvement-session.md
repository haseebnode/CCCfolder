# Example: Improving Landing Page Copy

## User Request
"This landing page isn't converting. Can you help improve it?"

## Original Copy

**Headline**: "Project Management Software for Teams"

**Subheadline**: "Manage your projects efficiently with our all-in-one platform. Features include task management, time tracking, and team collaboration."

**CTA**: "Sign Up"

---

## Claude Code Session

**User**: `/improve-copy`

### Step 1: Analysis

**Claude**: Let me analyze this copy. First, a few questions:

1. Who's your target customer? (role, company size, industry)
2. What's the #1 problem this solves?
3. What's your current conversion rate?
4. What happens after someone signs up?

**User**:
- Target: Marketing managers at 50-200 person companies
- Problem: They're drowning in Slack messages and lost spreadsheets, missing deadlines
- Conversion rate: 1.2% (goal is 3%)
- After signup: 14-day free trial, onboarding email sequence

### Step 2: Diagnosis

**Claude**: I see several issues:

❌ **Headline is generic**
- "Project management software" describes category, not benefit
- Could apply to 100 other tools
- Doesn't speak to specific pain point

❌ **Subheadline lists features, not benefits**
- "Task management" = feature
- What outcome does that create?
- Doesn't address emotional pain

❌ **CTA is weak**
- "Sign Up" = commitment, friction
- No indication of value or what happens next
- Missing risk reversal

❌ **No social proof visible**
- Nothing to build trust
- No indication others use this

❌ **Missing urgency or FOMO**
- No reason to act now vs. later

### Step 3: Improved Version

**Claude**: Here's an improved version:

---

**Headline**:
"Never Miss Another Deadline Because Someone 'Didn't See the Slack Message'"

**Subheadline**:
"The project management tool marketing teams actually use. Turn chaos into clarity with one source of truth for all your campaigns, deadlines, and deliverables."

**CTA Button**:
"Start Free 14-Day Trial"
*(No credit card required)*

**Supporting Copy**:
- ✅ Set up in under 5 minutes
- ✅ Free for 14 days, cancel anytime
- ✅ Join 2,000+ marketing teams

**Social Proof (add above CTA)**:
> "We went from missing 30% of deadlines to shipping everything on time. Game changer."
> — Sarah Chen, Marketing Director at TechCo

---

### Step 4: Explanation of Changes

**Claude**: Here's what I changed and why:

**1. Headline: Generic → Specific Pain**
- **Before**: "Project Management Software for Teams"
- **After**: "Never Miss Another Deadline Because Someone 'Didn't See the Slack Message'"
- **Why**: Instantly relatable problem. Marketing managers will think "that's me!"

**2. Subheadline: Features → Benefits**
- **Before**: Lists features (task management, time tracking)
- **After**: Describes transformation (chaos → clarity)
- **Why**: Benefits sell, features tell. "One source of truth" solves the core problem.

**3. Target Audience Called Out**
- **Added**: "marketing teams"
- **Why**: Specificity increases conversion. They know it's for them.

**4. CTA Strengthened**
- **Before**: "Sign Up" (scary commitment)
- **After**: "Start Free 14-Day Trial" (low risk, clear value)
- **Why**: Removes friction, sets expectations, includes risk reversal

**5. Trust Indicators Added**
- **Before**: Nothing
- **After**: Testimonial + social proof numbers
- **Why**: Builds credibility, shows others succeeded

### Step 5: A/B Test Suggestions

**Claude**: I recommend testing these variations:

**Test 1: Headline Variations**
- Version A (above): "Never Miss Another Deadline..."
- Version B: "Marketing Managers: Stop Chasing Updates in 17 Different Slack Channels"
- Version C: "Ship Campaigns On Time, Every Time—Without the Chaos"

**Test 2: CTA Variations**
- Version A: "Start Free 14-Day Trial"
- Version B: "See Your Projects in One Place"
- Version C: "Get Organized in 5 Minutes"

**Test 3: Social Proof Position**
- Version A: Above CTA (more prominent)
- Version B: Below CTA (less friction)

### Step 6: Expected Impact

**Claude**: Based on these changes, here's what should improve:

**Immediate Impact**:
- ✅ **Bounce rate** should decrease (headline hooks attention)
- ✅ **Time on page** should increase (copy is more engaging)
- ✅ **CTA clicks** should increase (lower friction, clearer value)

**Conversion Impact** (predicted):
- Current: 1.2%
- Target: 3.0%
- Conservative estimate: 2.2-2.5% (+83-108%)

**Why**:
- Specific pain point = qualified traffic stays
- Clear benefit = more understand value
- Reduced friction = more try it

**Timeline**: 2 weeks minimum to gather significant data (need ~1000 visitors for statistical significance)

## Key Lessons from This Session

1. **Ask context questions** before improving copy
2. **Diagnose problems** before suggesting solutions
3. **Explain the "why"** behind each change
4. **Provide A/B test variations** for optimization
5. **Set realistic expectations** for impact
6. **Create actionable next steps**
