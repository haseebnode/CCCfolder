# Landing Page Copy Template

## Hero Section

### Headline
**Formula**: [Desired Outcome] without [Main Objection]

**Examples**:
- "Get More Qualified Leads Without Spending More on Ads"
- "Launch Your Product in 30 Days Without Technical Headaches"
- "Scale to $100K MRR Without Hiring a Sales Team"

### Subheadline
Expand on the promise with specifics:
"[Specific mechanism] that helps [target audience] [achieve result] in [timeframe]"

### CTA Button
- Primary: [Action-Oriented Text] (e.g., "Start Free Trial", "Get My Custom Plan")
- Secondary: [Low-commitment option] (e.g., "See How It Works")

### Hero Image/Video
- Show the product in action
- Or: Happy customer using product
- Or: Before/after visual

## Design Notes
- **Above the fold**: Hero section must be compelling
- **Visual hierarchy**: Use size, color, whitespace to guide eye
- **Scannability**: Break text into chunks, use bullets
- **Mobile-first**: 60%+ traffic is mobile
- **Load speed**: Under 3 seconds or lose visitors
- **Single focus**: One clear goal, remove distractions
