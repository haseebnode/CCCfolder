# Cold Email Template

## Subject Lines (Pick One)
- {{pain_point}} solution for {{company_name}}
- Quick question about {{specific_challenge}}
- {{mutual_connection}} suggested I reach out

## Email Body

Hi {{first_name}},

**[Pattern Interrupt - personalized observation]**
I noticed {{specific_observation_about_their_company}}.

**[Problem Statement]**
Most {{job_title}}s I talk to struggle with {{specific_pain_point}}, which leads to {{negative_consequence}}.

**[Solution Tease]**
We help companies like {{competitor_or_similar_company}} {{specific_benefit}} by {{unique_mechanism}}.

**[Social Proof]**
For example, {{client_name}} saw {{specific_metric}} in {{timeframe}}.

**[Soft CTA]**
Worth a 15-minute conversation?

Best,
{{sender_name}}

---

## Personalization Tokens
- `{{first_name}}` - Lead's first name
- `{{company_name}}` - Company name
- `{{job_title}}` - Their role
- `{{pain_point}}` - Specific problem they face
- `{{specific_observation}}` - Custom research note

## Best Practices
- Keep under 150 words
- One clear CTA
- Personalize first line with research
- A/B test subject lines
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am recipient timezone
