# Nurture Sequence Template (5 Emails)

## Email 1: Welcome & Set Expectations
**Timing**: Immediately after signup
**Subject**: Welcome to {{company_name}} - here's what's next

Hi {{first_name}},

Thanks for joining! Here's what you can expect from us:
- [Benefit 1]
- [Benefit 2]
- [Benefit 3]

To get started, [simple first action].

**CTA**: [Get Started Button]

---

## Email 2: Education & Value
**Timing**: 2 days after Email 1
**Subject**: How {{customer_type}} use {{product_name}}

{{first_name}},

Most people don't realize [surprising insight about their problem].

Here's how [similar customer] used our [solution] to [achieve specific result]:
[Brief case study or story]

**Key takeaway**: [Main lesson]

**CTA**: [Learn More / Read Full Story]

---

## Email 3: Overcome Objection
**Timing**: 4 days after Email 2
**Subject**: "But what about [common objection]?"

Hi {{first_name}},

I hear this question a lot: "[common objection or concern]"

Fair question. Here's the truth:
[Address objection with data, story, or proof]

[Customer quote supporting your point]

**CTA**: [See How It Works]

---

## Email 4: Social Proof
**Timing**: 3 days after Email 3
**Subject**: How {{similar_company}} achieved {{big_result}}

{{first_name}},

{{similar_company}} faced the same challenge you might be facing: [problem].

Here's what they did:
- [Step 1 and result]
- [Step 2 and result]
- [Step 3 and result]

**Bottom line**: [Impressive outcome with metrics]

**CTA**: [Get Similar Results]

---

## Email 5: Clear CTA with Urgency
**Timing**: 5 days after Email 4
**Subject**: Ready to [achieve desired outcome]?

Hi {{first_name}},

Over the past few weeks, you've learned:
✓ [Key point from Email 2]
✓ [Key point from Email 3]
✓ [Key point from Email 4]

The next step is simple: [clear action step]

[Time-sensitive incentive or reason to act now]

**CTA**: [Strong Action Button]

P.S. [Additional nudge or bonus]

---

## Sequence Settings
- **Total Duration**: 2 weeks
- **Unsubscribe**: After Email 5 if no engagement
- **Re-engagement**: If opens but no clicks, send variation
- **Segmentation**: Tag based on which links they click
